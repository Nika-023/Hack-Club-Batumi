Manifesto
HOW WE OPERATE

Six rules that define everyone in this club.

01
You ship or you leave
Every member contributes to at least one shipped project per quarter. No exceptions.
02
No hand-holding
You are expected to be self-sufficient. Teammates help, but nobody carries you.
03
Real projects only
Everything we build is open source, deployed, or submitted to a real competition.
04
Win as a collective
Every hackathon, every competition — we enter under one name. Batumi wins.
05
Prove yourself first
Everyone goes through the same exam regardless of reputation. Output speaks.
06
Be obsessive
We want people who stay up until 2am because they can't stop. That energy wins.
